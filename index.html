FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are the invoices GST-valid?+
Yes. INVOX generates valid tax invoices per GST/IGST rules — with HSN codes and tax breakup.
Is my data safe?+
Yes. Data is backed up to the cloud and each user’s data stays isolated. Login is secured with OTP.
Does it work without internet?+
Yes. INVOX is an installable app (PWA) — create bills offline and they sync automatically once you’re back online.
